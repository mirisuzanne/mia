------

The Dirt Circle Dogs were an indi-rock band
that formed in Goshenm, IN
before moving to Denver, CO.
The band featured Daniel Eisenstat,
Sondra Eby,
Aaron Liechty,
and Miriam Suzanne.

Daniel, Sondra, and Miriam have since become
[Teacup Gorilla](teacup-gorilla/).
