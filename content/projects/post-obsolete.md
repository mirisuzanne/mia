The Post-Obsolete Book is
a [performable website][post-obsolete]
in the spirit (and tutelage)
of [Michelle Ellsworth][michelle] --
with text-to-speech voice-over,
transparencies,
party hats,
diagrams,
whiskey,
handouts,
and music by [The Widow's Bane][bane].
